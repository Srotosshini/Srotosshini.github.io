Journal Here Track Your Mood Memory Lane Self-Care Checklist

"Solace" was created by Maliha Nahiyan Srotosshini, a burnt-out freshman at NYU Abu Dhabi who decided to find joy in the little things. Through moments of mindfulness, journaling, and tracking her moods, Maliha embarked on a journey toward better mental health. This website is a manifestation of that journey, providing tools and resources for others to find their own solace. Solace aims to provide a supportive environment with a variety of tools, including journaling, mood tracking, self-care tips, and creative outlets like a coloring book. We believe in the power of small, consistent actions to bring about significant change in mental wellbeing. Join us in this journey, explore the resources we offer, and take your first step towards finding your own solace.
